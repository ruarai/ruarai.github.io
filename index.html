Ruarai Tobin

I'm a PhD candidate in infectious disease modelling in the Infectious Disease Dynamics Unit at the University of Melbourne. My PhD is focused on integrating the complexities of the adaptive immune response into respiratory virus transmission models.

Prior to the commencement of my PhD, I worked on modelling Plasmodium knowlesi malaria risk in Asia and supporting Australia's public health response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

My publications can be found below, or on Google Scholar.
