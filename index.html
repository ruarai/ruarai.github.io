I'm a PhD candidate in infectious disease modelling in the Infectious Disease Dynamics Unit at the University of Melbourne. My PhD is focused on integrating the complexities of the adaptive immune response into models of respiratory virus transmission.

Prior to the commencement of my PhD, I worked on modelling Plasmodium knowlesi malaria risk in Southeast Asia and supporting Australia's public health response to the COVID-19 pandemic.
